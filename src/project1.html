About me
A Journey Back to Childhood Curiosity
Ever since my early days, I've been captivated by the small wonders of the natural world—fish gliding beneath pond surfaces, frogs serenading the twilight, and birds soaring across the sky. My childhood was a tapestry of endless questions and explorations, driven by a deep desire to understand how animals perceive their environments and what their worlds look like.
While the days of my youth are behind me, the curiosity they fostered is not. This project began as a tribute to that spirit of inquiry and discovery. It's designed to bridge the gap between children's innate curiosity and their understanding of nature. In this project, my goal is to craft enriching exploratory experiences that offer children a more profound pathway to discovery the world in the eyes of different animals.
Why Educational Toy?
The best way for children to learn is through play. Children are naturally curious, and when they engage in playful activities, they are more likely to absorb new information and concepts. By designing an educational toy for children aged 5-12, I aim to leverage this natural inclination.
This toy will allow them to build their own animal models and then interact with these models from a first-person perspective, enabling them to explore the world as if they were animals. Through this fun and immersive experience, children will not only learn about animals but also gain a deeper understanding of the world around them.
